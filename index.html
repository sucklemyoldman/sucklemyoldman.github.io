I SUCKLE MY OLD MAN 

I 'Suckle My Old Man' is a story of betrayal and benevolence, climaxing in a love revelation that conquers all blunders of the past and heals the deepest of wounds....


Author

Feji Khai, young and emerging Nigerian author, writer extraordinaire speaks on her new book;,

"I SUCKLE MY OLD MAN is a story every one can relate to.

Humanity is frail and people sometimes go through some overwhelming negative experiences forced on them or caused by them.

Some don't ever get over hurts, not because they don't want to, but because they don't know how to and interestingly some feel they have healed when they are actually still bleeding.

I suckle my old man is a story of love in its truest form - unconditional, honest and open. In its expression is a deep betrayal, an unwavering support and faith that will not let go. This love culminates in forgiveness, healing, and a turn of events that can only be divine.

I promise you great entertainment as you explore the pages of this book..see you at the end

Feji Khai
